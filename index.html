Overview

UAVLight is designed to study how outdoor 3D reconstruction methods behave under significant illumination changes. The dataset captures large-scale real-world UAV scenes across multiple lighting conditions, enabling evaluation beyond the conventional single-time acquisition setup.

Existing outdoor reconstruction datasets often assume relatively stable appearance and limited illumination variation. In contrast, UAVLight emphasizes challenging real-world conditions such as morning, noon, afternoon, and other time-dependent lighting changes, providing a dedicated testbed for illumination-aware modeling, relighting, and robust reconstruction.
